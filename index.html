Who we are

We are a small team of developers based in San Pedro Sula, Honduras. We are specialised in JavaScript, AngularJS, ASP.NET, Node.js. Objective-C for building web and mobile apps. Our main development process is managed using Scrum.

Skilled team

Our developers have 8+ years of experience working in different industries. They have the capacity to understand the business needs to build the best solution.

Agile approach

We love changes and so we follow scrum as our main agile software development practice. To make sure we follow it, we have a ScrumMaster certified by ScrumAlliance to guide us.

High quality code

We value clean code and we follow SOLID principles. To make sure we do this we like to have continuous integration as part of our development process, we set different development environments and we test our code..
